Hello! I'm Dave Kimura

I'm a Ruby on Rails developer with over 16 years of experience in building web applications. I love Ruby and Rails for its expressiveness, productivity and elegance. I enjoy creating user-friendly, scalable and secure solutions for various domains, such as e-commerce, education, social media and more.

I'm also passionate about sharing my knowledge and insights with the Ruby community. I'm the creator of Drifting Ruby, a Ruby on Rails screencast series that covers various topics and techniques for web development. I'm also a panelist on the Ruby Rogues podcast, where I discuss Ruby news, tips and tricks with other Ruby experts.

This website showcases some of my articles and projects that I'm proud of. Feel free to contact me if you have any questions or inquiries. I'm always open to new challenges and opportunities.
